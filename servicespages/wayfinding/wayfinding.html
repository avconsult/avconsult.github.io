What is Wayfinding?

Through wayfinding, we model your business premises, be it comprised of a single building block or multiple ones, and create simulated walkthroughs from any source to any destination.

These walkthroughs are then accessible either through clicking on a building block or area in your site's rendering, or through menu and text with search options, making it easy for anyone to go anywhere.
